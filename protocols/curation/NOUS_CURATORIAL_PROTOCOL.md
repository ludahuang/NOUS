# NOUS Curatorial Protocol

Status: draft 0.1

## Mechanism

1. **Question** - state the tension, curiosity, or future possibility.
2. **Temporary interface** - choose a space, site, graph, agent, publication, or event.
3. **Invitation** - identify people, works, sources, and non-human participants.
4. **Encounter** - allow relationships and contradictions to become visible.
5. **Trace** - record decisions, works, media, source material, and context.
6. **Structuring** - convert the trace into catalog entities and relations.
7. **Transmission** - publish or package the result in a portable form.
8. **Reactivation** - allow a later site, curator, or agent to produce a new edition.

## Required Records

Every NOUS program should record:

- the initiating question;
- responsible people and agents;
- time and location;
- participating works and sources;
- generated outputs;
- rights and restrictions;
- relationships to earlier and later editions;
- preservation status.

## Curatorial Constraint

Reactivation is not silent duplication. A new edition should preserve its
lineage while stating what changed, who changed it, and under what conditions.
