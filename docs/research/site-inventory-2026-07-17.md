## Interpretation

The four domains should not be flattened into one scraped collection:

- `we-media.net` explains the organization and current agent direction;
- `nous.we-media.net` carries the strongest spatial and exhibition history;
- `weme.im` records a brand and service layer;
- `rebui1t.com` records active AI research and generated work.

The catalog should link these sources through entities and provenance before
any selective preservation capture begins.
