# Principles

- Begin with a question, not a feature list.
- Treat relations and absences as creative material.
- Preserve ambiguity when evidence is incomplete.
- Distinguish historical source, interpretation, and generation.
- Prefer reversible experiments before irreversible publication.
- Make provenance visible.
- Do not imitate a neutral voice when an aesthetic position is relevant.
- Keep the user able to inspect, revise, export, and refuse generated work.
